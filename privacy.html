Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: TARS is designed with privacy at its core. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our AI copilot extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our browser extension collects the following types of information to provide AI-powered automation services:

Website Content and Interaction Data

  • Page Content: Text, headings, form fields, buttons, and clickable elements from web pages you visit
  • URLs and Page Titles: Web addresses and titles of pages where you use the extension
  • User Commands: Instructions you provide to the AI assistant
  • Automation Actions: Records of clicks, form fills, and navigation performed by the extension
  • Screenshots: Occasional page screenshots for AI analysis when needed

Technical Data

  • Extension Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings
  • Conversation History: Your chat interactions with the AI assistant
  • Error Logs: Technical information to help diagnose and fix issues

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered browser automation and assistance
  • Understand web page content and structure for intelligent decision-making
  • Maintain conversation context and user preferences
  • Improve the extension's functionality and user experience
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and provide support

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

OpenAI Integration

To provide AI functionality, we share website content, user commands, and page context with OpenAI's API services. This data is transmitted securely and is subject to OpenAI's privacy policy and data usage terms.

Important: Your OpenAI API key is stored locally in your browser and never transmitted to our servers. You maintain full control over your AI usage and costs.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage
  • Encryption: Data transmitted to external services is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Retention: Local data persists until you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data
  • External Processing: Data sent to OpenAI for AI processing is handled according to their data retention policies

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Review what data is stored locally through the extension interface
  • Delete: Clear conversation history and settings through the extension options
  • Control: Choose which websites to use the extension on
  • Opt-out: Disable or uninstall the extension at any time
  • Export: Download your conversation history and settings data

6. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

When using OpenAI services, your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure that any international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place.

8. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will notify affected users promptly and take immediate steps to secure the data and prevent further unauthorized access.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We will also notify users of significant changes through the extension interface. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@tarsai.com
Website: https://tarsai.com
Response Time: We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours

11. Compliance and Certifications

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Commitment to Privacy: We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. By using the TARS AI Copilot Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.
